COVDI-19 Database

World and US Data

On Dec 31 2019, Wuhan, China reported to CDC a cluster of cases of pneumonia in China. A novel type of cornovirus virus was soon identified. In winter of 2020, the virus outbreak went off in China and millions of people were suffering. After months of medical professionals and people fighting against the virus, the spreading in China ceased. However, the virus started spreading across the world, causing a global pandemic. Right now, the United States became one of the most serious regions affected by the coronavirus; people are losing jobs, schools are shutting down, cities that were full of lights and crowds are now fall silent. This database is created to help provide better visualization of the coronavirus cases around the world as well as in the United States. The data came from reliable sources. For details, click Here.
